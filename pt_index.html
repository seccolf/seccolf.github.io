Olá, eu sou Lucas F. Secco

I'm interested in understanding the Universe through the lens of science.

About me

I'm currently a PhD Candidate at the University of Pennsylvania, expecting to graduate by Summer 2020. I hold a Master's degree in Physics from Universidade de São Paulo (2015) and a Bachelor's degree in Physics from Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul (2013). You can check out my complete CV here: LINK.

I'm originally from Brazil and care about how science (and scientists) can contribute to social progress by fueling a culture of humanism, skepticism and intellectual honesty. In practice, I try to achieve that by blogging (link to Medium) and tweeting (link to twittes) about science and politics.
